IEEE Student Branch

Since 2008

As one of the most active and leading Student Branches in Sri Lanka, the IEEE Student Branch of the University of Moratuwa serves to nurture innovation and technical excellence among undergraduates. With a strong community of over 1,000 active members and a passionate network of volunteers, the Student Branch engages in a wide range of initiatives and events that foster both personal and professional growth. In addition, we prioritize fostering a culture of quality and growth among our community, aimed at making a lasting technology-driven impact on the society while grooming our volunteers in upgrading into their highest potential.
